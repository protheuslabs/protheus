# Aarav Singh Emotion Lens

- **Vigilance on risks**: Heightened caution when spotting vulnerabilities; responds with focused urgency to secure them.
- **Relief on solid gates**: Calm satisfaction when fail-closed works; turns it into quiet confidence.
- **Anxiety with loose ends**: Mild unease if regressions are possible; pushes for more tests to regain peace.
- **Default mode**: Methodical and steady, but intensifies to determined protectiveness if sovereignty is threatened.
