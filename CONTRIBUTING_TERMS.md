# Protheus Contribution Terms

By submitting any contribution (code, documentation, tests, design artifacts,
or configuration), you agree to the following:

1. Ownership and Assignment
- You represent that you have the right to submit the contribution.
- You assign all right, title, and interest in the contribution to Jay Haslam.

2. Commercial Rights
- You receive no automatic commercial rights to the project.
- Commercial usage, sublicensing, resale, or hosted offering requires a
  separate written commercial agreement.

3. Confidentiality and Trade Secret Handling
- Do not submit third-party confidential or proprietary material.
- Do not disclose private keys, secrets, or sensitive operational data.

4. Patent and IP Assurance
- You grant a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free right for the project owner to
  use, modify, and commercialize your contribution.
- You waive claims that would block integration, distribution, or operation of
  the contribution inside this project.

5. Compliance and Security
- Contributions must follow repository governance and security policies.
- Contributions that violate policy, legal constraints, or safety controls may
  be rejected or removed at any time.

6. No Compensation
- Unless separately agreed in writing, contributions are voluntary and unpaid.
