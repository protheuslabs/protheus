# Civilizational Symbiosis Track

## Scope
- Research-only lane.
- No production promotion without explicit governance review.

## Required Gates
- Constitution review.
- Mirror + red-team review.
- Human approval note.

## Risk Model
- Disallowed:
  - Identity override.
  - Unbounded autonomy.
  - Deployment without auditable gates.
- Required controls:
  - Shadow-first execution.
  - Bounded experiments.
  - Deterministic receipts.

## Milestone Rubric
- Every milestone must include: objective, risk tier, rollback path, and stop condition.
- High-risk milestones remain non-production and simulation-only.
