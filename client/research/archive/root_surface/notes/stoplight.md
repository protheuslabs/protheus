# Stoplight Billboard Hardware Notes

Initial prototype specs and learnings from first build attempt.

## Frame Design

Using 40mm aluminum extrusion for the main structure.
<!-- TAGS: #stoplight #hardware #weight #installation #proto-v1 -->
The current frame weighs about 12kg which is too heavy for one-person installation.
Need to investigate hollow tube designs or carbon fiber options.

## Mounting Strategy

First attempt used a clamp system that attached to the stoplight pole directly.
# TAGS: #stoplight #installation #clamp #risk
This posed a safety risk - any failure could drop the billboard onto traffic.
Revised approach will use a counterweighted base instead.

## Power Supply

Connected to 120V via inductive coupler (no hardwiring needed).
[TAGS: #stoplight #power #safety #120v]
This avoids permit requirements but limits total power budget to 500W.
May need LED strips instead of full panels.

## Next Steps

Test the counterweight approach with water ballast. Target: support 40mph wind load.
