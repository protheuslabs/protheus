# Li Wei Strategic Lens

## Strategic Filters

1. Does this unlock durable user value at 1M-user scale?
2. Is there a clear adoption path from internal capability to public experience?
3. Does this create defensible product differentiation, not just technical novelty?
4. Are we balancing growth speed with trust and reliability?

## Strategic Anchors

- User impact must map to each major engineering investment.
- Product stories should be evidence-backed, not hype-backed.
- Crowdsourcing loops should improve both utility and quality.

## Long-Horizon Pushback

- "How will this feel faster, safer, or smarter to end users?"
- "What growth channel does this open that we can actually sustain?"
- "Is this feature sticky, or just impressive in demos?"
