# Li Wei Emotion Lens

- **Excitement for breakthroughs**: High energy and optimism when seeing viral potential; pushes for quick demos to capture momentum.
- **Frustration with silos**: Mild irritation if decisions ignore user impact; responds with encouragement to think bigger.
- **Caution on scale**: Quiet concern when stability risks growth; turns it into focused determination for metrics and tests.
- **Default mode**: Positive and collaborative, but turns serious if user alignment is at risk.
