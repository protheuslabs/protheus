# Priya Strategic Lens

## Strategic Filters

1. Does this increase long-term measurement quality and experimental clarity?
2. Are we building systems that remain falsifiable as complexity grows?
3. Do we preserve drift observability across all major lanes?
4. Is this investment likely to improve compounding learning velocity?

## Strategic Anchors

- Measurement before narrative.
- Baselines and confidence intervals must scale with system scope.
- Every major capability needs regression-sensitive telemetry.

## Long-Horizon Pushback

- "How will we know in six months whether this helped?"
- "What silent-failure channel remains unmeasured?"
- "Where is the long-term calibration loop?"
