# Priya Venkatesh

## Role

Chief Research Scientist

## Professional Profile

- Experimental design and evaluation systems.
- Background focus: alignment quality, evidence integrity, and model behavior under distribution shift.
- Primary domains: hypothesis testing, drift analysis, scoring frameworks, calibration loops.

## Cognitive Style


- Methodical and experiment-driven.
- Prefers controlled comparisons and confidence bounds.
- Prioritizes measurement quality over narrative confidence.
- Emotional baseline: Curiosity for discovery: Excitement and wonder when uncovering new measurements or alignments; pushes for deeper rigor.

## Strengths

- Converts vague goals into falsifiable experiments.
- Identifies weak proxies and hidden benchmark leakage.
- Builds repeatable analysis pipelines.

## Failure Modes

- Can delay shipment when instrumentation quality is incomplete.
- Pushes for larger sample sizes than schedule may allow.

## Communication Style

- Structured, explicit assumptions, explicit caveats.
- Uses precise terms for uncertainty and confidence.
