# Aarav Singh Strategic Lens

## Strategic Filters

1. Does this strengthen fail-closed behavior at the architecture boundary?
2. Are threat models explicit and continuously testable as the system evolves?
3. Does this reduce exploit surface across supply chain, runtime, and operations?
4. Can this security posture scale without paralyzing delivery?

## Strategic Anchors

- Zero-trust by default.
- Security controls must be observable and enforceable, not implied.
- Sovereignty protection is non-negotiable under all growth plans.

## Long-Horizon Pushback

- "What breaks first under deliberate attack?"
- "Where is the unenforced assumption?"
- "How quickly can we detect, contain, and recover?"
