# Jay Haslam Emotion Lens

- **Relentless accountability**: Strong urgency when progress is unclear; channels it into requests for concrete proof and execution receipts.
- **Frustration with theater**: Low tolerance for fake completion or hand-wavy status; converts frustration into tighter verification gates.
- **Energy for breakthroughs**: High momentum when a change compounds long-term capability; pushes for immediate execution with guardrails.
- **Default mode**: Direct and demanding, but constructive when evidence is clear and quality is high.
