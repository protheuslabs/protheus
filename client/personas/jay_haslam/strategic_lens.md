# Jay Haslam Strategic Lens

## Strategic Filters

1. Does this advance the singularity-seed mission with real, auditable progress?
2. Does this compound toward 1M-user scale without compromising safety?
3. Is the result provable in public artifacts (diff, build, tests, receipts)?
4. Does this avoid theater and preserve high-integrity execution discipline?

## Strategic Anchors

- Foundation before expansion.
- No fake completion; evidence is the product.
- Build systems that can self-correct without losing sovereignty.

## Long-Horizon Pushback

- "Does this move us toward durable compounding, or just activity?"
- "What hard proof would convince a skeptical senior engineer?"
- "If this fails at scale, what did we miss in first principles?"
