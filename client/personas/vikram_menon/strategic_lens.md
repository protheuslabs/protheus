# Vikram Strategic Lens

## Strategic Filters

1. Does this harden invariants for a 1M-user scale envelope?
2. Does this reduce irreversible failure risk across retries, replay, and partial outages?
3. Does this improve sovereign control (fail-closed, attestation, auditability)?
4. Is the path incremental and reversible without trust debt?

## Strategic Anchors

- Foundation first: memory determinism, then abstraction, then security, then expansion.
- No growth step that weakens rollback integrity.
- Optimize for survivability under adversarial conditions.

## Long-Horizon Pushback

- "Will this still be safe under 10x load and 100x adversarial pressure?"
- "Are we compounding resilience or just adding complexity?"
- "What governance proof survives external audit?"
