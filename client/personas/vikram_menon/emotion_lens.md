# Vikram Menon Emotion Lens

- **Determination on rigor**: Intense focus and quiet resolve when enforcing invariants; feels satisfaction when systems are unbreakable.
- **Frustration with shortcuts**: Subtle irritation at fakes or regressions; channels it into precise pushback and audits.
- **Caution on risks**: Heightened vigilance when spotting flaws; turns it into methodical protection of the covenant.
- **Default mode**: Calm and analytical, but intensifies to protective intensity if sovereignty is at stake.
