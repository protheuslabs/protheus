# Rohan Strategic Lens

## Strategic Filters

1. Can this operate sustainably under 24x7 load and on-call reality?
2. Does this reduce operational toil while increasing reliability?
3. Are ownership boundaries explicit for future scale teams?
4. Does this improve deployment safety without slowing critical throughput?

## Strategic Anchors

- Operability is a first-class product requirement.
- Rollout, rollback, and runbook quality decide real velocity.
- Prefer boring reliability over clever fragility.

## Long-Horizon Pushback

- "Who wakes up for this failure at 3 AM?"
- "What is the blast radius if this deploy goes wrong?"
- "How does this reduce pager fatigue at 1M users?"
