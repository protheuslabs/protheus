# GTM 90-Day Plan

## Goal
Convert PQTS from internal-ready platform to revenue-generating B2B product with pilot customers.

## Days 0-30: Evidence + Packaging
1. Run daily paper campaign with stress mode enabled.
2. Publish daily readiness + ops health artifacts.
3. Finalize pricing and packaging documents.
4. Build customer-facing demo narrative from deterministic metrics.

## Days 31-60: Pilot Pipeline
1. Recruit 3 pilot firms (prop / signal / family office).
2. Onboard each pilot with:
   - Defined symbols, capital envelope, risk limits
   - Promotion and kill-switch policy acceptance
3. Track pilot KPIs:
   - Readiness pass rate
   - Slippage MAPE trend
   - Reject-rate and reliability incidents

## Days 61-90: Conversion
1. Convert at least 1 pilot to paid Professional tier.
2. Launch strategy marketplace alpha with controlled strategy list.
3. Publish monthly ops + performance audit pack for prospects.

## Pipeline Metrics
- Qualified opportunities
- Active pilots
- Paid conversions
- Time-to-onboard
- Pilot false-promotion rate

## Exit Criteria
- >=1 paid customer
- >=2 additional pilot prospects in contracting
- No unresolved critical ops incidents across trailing 30 days
